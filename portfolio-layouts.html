Layout 2

Justified Rows

Every row stretches edge-to-edge, with images at different widths based on their natural proportions. Rows alternate between tall and short heights for visual rhythm. Clean and dense like a gallery wall.

Strengths Clean, edge-to-edge density Natural reading order (left to right) Varying row heights add rhythm
Considerations Some images get cropped Last row can look odd if incomplete

Layout 5

Scattered / Collage

Images feel casually placed, like prints on a table — slightly rotated, overlapping, with white borders. Warm, personal, and handcrafted. Perfect for the family photography brand.

Strengths Warm, personal, handcrafted Unique and memorable Perfect for family photography
Considerations Harder to make responsive Fixed positions need manual curation
Layout 6

Horizontal Filmstrip

A cinematic strip that scrolls sideways, like walking past a gallery wall. Images are tall and immersive. Drag or scroll horizontally to browse. Dramatic and elegant.

Scroll or drag horizontally to browse

Strengths Cinematic and immersive Images stay large and impactful Mixed sizes feel natural
Considerations Not all content visible at once Horizontal scroll can feel unfamiliar
Layout 7

Mixed-Weight Modular Grid

A structured grid where some images break out to double-width or double-height, creating visual variety while keeping an underlying rhythm. The best of both worlds — organized but not monotonous.

Strengths Structured but not monotonous Visual hierarchy within a grid Easy to add/remove images
Considerations Images get cropped to fit cells Need to decide which images are "featured"
