Common Questions

What should we wear?

Soft, neutral tones work best — think creams, tans, and muted greens. Avoid bold logos or busy patterns. I'll send you a full style guide once you book!

What if my baby won't stop crying?

Babies cry — it's totally normal! I build extra time into every session so we can pause for feedings, diaper changes, and cuddles. Patience is my superpower.

Can I bring my kids?

Absolutely! Siblings are family too. I love incorporating the whole family into sessions — some of my favorite shots include everyone together.

How long until I get my photos?

You'll receive your full gallery within 2-3 weeks. Each image is carefully edited to match the Little Image style. A viewing session will be scheduled so we can go through your favorites together.
